About Seshat

Named after the Egyptian goddess of writing, wisdom, and measurement - "She who scribes"

Seshat performs stylometric analysis to create unique "writing fingerprints" from text samples, enabling authorship attribution and psychological profiling based on linguistic markers.

Use Cases

  • Authorship verification and attribution
  • Psychological profiling from text
  • AI-generated content detection
  • Cross-platform author linking
  • Forensic text analysis

AI Detection

Detect if text is AI-generated or human-written

Longer texts produce more accurate results. Minimum 50 characters required.

How It Works

This tool analyzes stylometric features that differ between AI-generated and human-written text:

  • Punctuation Consistency: AI tends to be more consistent in punctuation patterns
  • Vocabulary Distribution: AI often has flatter vocabulary distributions
  • Phrase Patterns: AI may repeat similar structures more frequently
  • Hesitation Markers: Humans naturally use filler words and hesitations
  • Cognitive Load: Human writing shows signs of fatigue and variation

Note: AI detection is probabilistic and not 100% accurate. Use results as one data point among many.
